Welcome to Ducks Board Games

Every Friday the ducks gather at YaPeDaCa's or...Freiain's...to play board games. Some of our favourites include:

Patchwork
2 players compete to arrange quilt patches and collect the most buttons.
Codenames
2 teams compete to guess the correct words using minimal clues.
Werewolf
Villagers must deduce the identity of werewolves who are trying to kill them.
Betrayal At House On The Hill
A team explores a mansion and prepare for a battle against a traitor.
Inis
Players compete to become the king of a newly discovered land.
